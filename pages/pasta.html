Tomato Pasta Sauce

Is your spaghetti boring and dull? Try this super easy pasta sauce recipe to brighten up your dish! It's fast, tasty and only needs a few ingredients. So go on and try it out! Add it to your cooked pasta for a punch of flavor that you won't regret.

Ingredients

  • 6 fresh tomatos
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, finely minced
  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil
  • Parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Mix tomatoes, lemon juice, basil, pepper, and parsley in a bowl
  2. Heat olive oil in a saucepan
  3. Add the garlic and cook, stirring for 2 minutes
  4. Add onions and continue to cook and stir for 1 minute
  5. Grill until lightly browned and flip over
  6. Add the tomato mixture and salt and simmer for 10 minutes
